## Overview

Scrivener Advanced is a professional writing application designed specifically for long-form writing projects, providing a project-based environment with a corkboard, outliner, research folder, and manuscript compilation that authors, academics, and screenwriters need to manage complex multi-section works.

The corkboard displays scenes, chapters, and sections as index cards arranged in a spatial layout, enabling the kind of structural overview and reorganization that cannot be achieved by scrolling through a long single document.

Outline view presents the same content as a hierarchical tree with synopses, word counts, and custom metadata visible at a glance, supporting the structural planning and review that long-form writing requires before committing to a final organization.

Research folder keeps reference material — web pages, PDFs, images, notes — alongside the manuscript in the same project, making source material immediately accessible while writing without switching between applications or browser windows.

For novelists, academics, journalists, and screenwriters who work on long-form projects that require structural management beyond what a word processor provides, Scrivener Advanced is the writing environment built for exactly that purpose.

## Performance

Scrivener balances performance with system resource usage intelligently. Operations are handled in the background, keeping your Mac responsive for everything else. Efficient memory management and smart caching reduce overhead during extended work sessions.

Apple Silicon acceleration is fully utilized on M-series Macs, delivering noticeably faster performance. On Intel machines, hardware acceleration is used wherever available.
